Combat System

Mastering Combat

Oblivion's combat revolves around four weapon skills — Blade, Blunt, Hand to Hand, and Marksman — each tied to a governing attribute, Strength or Agility. The core of melee combat is blocking and countering: shield-block an enemy's strike, then attack during their recovery. Power Attacks, triggered by holding the attack button, deal heavy damage and can be directed by moving in different directions. Against ranged enemies and mages, strafing side to side throws off their aim, while a well-timed sidestep is the only reliable way to dodge magic. Keeping your weapons repaired is essential, as deteriorated gear can cut your damage output significantly.
