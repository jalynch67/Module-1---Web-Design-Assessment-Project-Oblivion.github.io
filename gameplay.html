Combat System

Mastering Combat

Oblivion's combat revolves around four weapon skills — Blade, Blunt, Hand to Hand, and Marksman — each tied to Strength or Agility. Blocking, timing, and counterattacks form the core of melee combat. Power attacks deal heavy damage and vary depending on movement direction. Against ranged enemies, strafing and timing are key to survival. Weapon durability also plays a major role in overall damage output.

Character Progression & Leveling

Leveling System

In Oblivion, your skills improve by using them in gameplay. Combat, magic, and stealth actions directly influence your character growth. When you rest, you level up and allocate attribute bonuses.

This system encourages organic development while still allowing strategic build optimization depending on how you play.

Magic & Alchemy

Arcane Systems

Magic is divided into schools such as Destruction, Restoration, Illusion, Conjuration, Alteration, and Mysticism. Each school offers unique abilities and playstyles.

Alchemy allows you to craft potions and poisons from ingredients found across the world. Experimentation reveals effects and enables powerful custom combinations.

Together, Magic and Alchemy create deep gameplay flexibility for any character build.
