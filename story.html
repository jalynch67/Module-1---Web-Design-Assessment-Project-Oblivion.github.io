The Story of Oblivion

Step into a world on the brink of destruction.

Main Story Journey

Follow the rise of a hero and the fall of an empire.

You begin as a prisoner, unexpectedly visited by Emperor Uriel Septim. Moments later, assassins strike, and you escape through hidden tunnels beneath the Imperial City.

With the Emperor dead, gates to Oblivion begin opening across the land, unleashing Daedric forces upon Tamriel.

You must find the last heir, Martin Septim, and help him reclaim the throne to stop the invasion.

In a final confrontation, Martin sacrifices himself to banish Mehrunes Dagon and save Tamriel forever.

Oblivion Gates

Oblivion Gates appear across Cyrodiil after the assassination of Emperor Uriel Septim VII. These portals lead directly into the Daedric realm of Oblivion, ruled by Mehrunes Dagon.

Inside each gate, the player must fight through Daedra and navigate shifting environments such as lava-filled landscapes and twisted towers. The goal is to reach the Sigil Stone at the top of the main tower and remove it to collapse the gate.

Closing these gates is essential to stopping the Daedric invasion and protecting nearby cities from destruction.

Battle of Kvatch

The Battle of Kvatch is one of the earliest major events in the main quest. The city is attacked after an Oblivion Gate opens nearby, allowing Daedra to overrun its defenses.

When the player arrives, most of Kvatch is already in ruins. Survivors have taken refuge inside the Chapel, while the city streets are overrun with Daedra.

The player fights alongside Captain Savlian Matius to reclaim the city and close the Oblivion Gate responsible for the attack.

The Final Sacrifice

At the climax of the main quest, Martin Septim, the last living heir of the Septim bloodline, confronts Mehrunes Dagon during his physical manifestation in the Imperial City.

With the Amulet of Kings destroyed, Martin uses the power of Akatosh to transform into a divine avatar, becoming the Dragon God of Time.

He defeats Mehrunes Dagon in the mortal realm, sealing the Oblivion Crisis permanently, but sacrifices himself in the process, ending the Septim bloodline and ushering in a new era for Tamriel.
